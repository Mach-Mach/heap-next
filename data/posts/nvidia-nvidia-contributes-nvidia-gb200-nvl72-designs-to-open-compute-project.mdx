During the 2024 OCP Global Summit, NVIDIA announced that it has contributed
the NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 rack and compute and switch tray liquid cooled designs
to the Open Compute Project (OCP). This post provides details about this
contribution and explains how it increases the utility of current design
standards to meet the high compute density demands of modern data centers.
